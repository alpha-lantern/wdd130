About Us - Whitewater Rafting

White Water Rating is simply the best in the business. Our guide, Rory, was knowledgeable, safety-focused, and hilarious, making the challenging Thunderclap River accessible and exciting. The equipment was top-notch, and the whole experience felt authentic, not commercialized. If you want a genuine, thrilling river trip with true experts who've been guiding since 1990, look no further. Highly recommend this company for an unforgettable adventure!

History

Our history starts back in 1990 with a group of adventure enthusiasts. Their love for adventure took them to different places where they started building a community of people like you and me! Word quickly spread about their top-tier guidance.

They built their reputation not on size, but on deep river knowledge and a commitment to safety and adventure. Decades later, while the company has grown, the original, hands-on passion for the rapids remains the core of White Water Rating.
